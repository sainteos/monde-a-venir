## Freedom is complicated
* Freedom as an idea is not as simple as most would like it to be
* There is freedom of, but there is also freedom from

### Freedom Of
1. Speech
2. Assembly
3. Life
4. Death
5. Housing
6. Healthcare
7. Religion/Spirituality
8. Association
9. Healthcare
10. Voting
11. Ideas

### Freedom From
1. Murder
2. Violence
3. Threat
4. Coercion
5. Nationalism
6. Eugenics
7. Disease
